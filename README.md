# Privacy Policy

**Footy Scores**
Last updated: 2 April 2026

## Overview

Footy Scores is a free AFL scores and ladder app developed by Adam Carbone. Your privacy is important — this app is designed to collect as little data as possible.

## Data Collection

**Footy Scores does not collect, store, or share any personal information.**

The app stores the following preferences locally on your device only:

- Theme preference (System, Light, or Dark)
- Favourite team selection
- Notification settings

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to the developer or any third party.

## Network Requests

The app connects to a backend server to retrieve live AFL scores, fixtures, and ladder data. This data is sourced from the [Squiggle API](https://api.squiggle.com.au). These requests do not include any personal or identifying information.

## Third-Party Services

The app does not use any analytics, advertising, tracking, or crash reporting services.

## Data Sharing

No data is shared with third parties.

## Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any data from children or any other users.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
